Detection of Both SA0 and SA1 Faults on a 4-Bit EVEN Parity Checker Circuit

Aim


  • To detect both SA0 (stuck-at-0) and SA1 (stuck-at-1) faults on a 4-bit EVEN parity checker circuit.
  • To verify the truth table of a 4-bit EVEN parity checker circuit and observe the output of the circuit with faults set at required lines.
